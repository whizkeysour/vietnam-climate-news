# Vietnam Climate News Metadata

This repository contains metadata of Vietnamese news articles focusing on **climate change**, **environmental pollution**, and **renewable energy**. The dataset includes articles from major Vietnamese news outlets (VTV, VnExpress, Nhân Dân), collected for research purposes.

> **Note:** Full article text is **not included** due to copyright restrictions. Only metadata (URL, title, date) is provided.

## Dataset

- **File:** `vietnam_climate_news_metadata.json`  
- **Fields:**
  - `url` – URL of the article  
  - `title` – Title of the article  
  - `date` – Publication date
